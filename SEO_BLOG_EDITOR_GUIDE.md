## Blog Editor Fields

### Basic Content Fields

**Title*** (Required)
- The main heading of your blog post
- This is displayed prominently on the page and in search results

**Slug*** (Required)
- URL-friendly version of the title
- Auto-generated from title, but can be customized
- Example: `eaton-fire-anniversary-survivors-recovery`
- Used in the URL: `yoursite.com/blog/[slug]`

**Excerpt** (Optional)
- Brief summary of the post (1-2 sentences)
- Displayed in blog listings

**Content (Markdown)*** (Required)
- Main blog post content
- Write in **Markdown** format for formatting:
  - `# Heading 1`, `## Heading 2`, `### Heading 3`
  - `**bold text**` and `*italic text*`
  - `- bullet points` and `1. numbered lists`
  - `[link text](https://url.com)`
  - `![alt text](/path/to/image.jpg)`

**Cover Image URL** (Optional)
- Full URL to the featured/cover image
- Example: `https://yoursite.com/public/Images/image1.webp`
- Recommended size: 1200x630px for best quality on social media

```markdown

```

### Text Formatting

## Troubleshooting

### "Magic Link Not Received"
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify the email address is correct and authorized
- Contact Earl if your email isn't on the allowlist

### "Content Not Saving"
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure all required fields are filled (Title, Content, Slug)
- Check the error message displayed on the page
- Try again after a few moments

### "Preview Looks Different"
- The preview uses live styling once published
- Draft posts may appear differently
- Allow 1-2 minutes for the site to update after publishing
